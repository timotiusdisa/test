About this property

Nestled in the heart of Seminyak's most coveted stretch, Monoloog Bali Seminyak is a sanctuary of refined elegance and authentic Balinese spirit. Set across two acres of lush tropical gardens, the hotel seamlessly blends contemporary design with traditional craftsmanship — hand-carved stone facades, cascading water features, and locally-sourced teak furnishings that tell the story of the island.

Each of our 87 rooms and suites opens directly onto verdant greenery or our award-winning infinity pool, with unobstructed views of the Indian Ocean at sunset. The design philosophy follows the Balinese concept of Tri Hita Karana — harmony between people, nature, and the divine — creating spaces that feel simultaneously modern and deeply rooted in place.

Wake to the sound of gamelan at our open-air breakfast pavilion. Unwind at our spa with traditional Balinese healing rituals. Watch the sun dip below the horizon from our rooftop bar. Every moment at Monoloog Seminyak is designed to linger in memory long after you leave.
